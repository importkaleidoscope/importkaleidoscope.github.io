About us
Import Kaleidoscope was established in 1995 as an import and export company specialising in the sourcing and distribution of various quality products locally and abroad. We have since grown into one of the leading procurement companies of products supplying the Petro-Chemical, Mining, Cement and Construction Industries. 
Import Kaleidoscope is a one-stop solution provider. We provide effective solutions in the sourcing and supply of a wide and extensive range of products, parts & equipment, with emphasis on distinguished brands known for its quality and reliability. To ensure the highest levels of service and the shortest possible delivery times, we utilize a well established international network of manufacturers and suppliers from South Africa, Europe, the U.S.A. and the Far East.
The Canadian based company SPRUNG, the industry leader in tension membrane structures, has appointed Import Kaleidoscope as their International Exclusive Representative in Africa.
